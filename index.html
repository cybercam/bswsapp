Features

What's Inside

Not another reading app. This one helps you actually retain what you read.

Spaced Repetition (SM2)

The algorithm schedules your next review right before you'd forget. Four modes: flashcard, fill-in-the-blank, first-letter, and full typing.

Your Mother Tongue

Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, Odia and more — alongside ESV, NKJV, and KJV English.

Parallel Bible

Compare any two versions side by side. Read in English while studying in your heart language, or cross-check translations.

Quiz & Leaderboard

Multiple choice, guess-the-reference, and verse completion. See how you rank against other believers.

Hebrew & Greek Interlinear

Tap any word for Strong's numbers, original definitions, transliteration, and morphology breakdown.

Commentary in Your Language

Chapter summaries, verse explanations, and cross-references — translated into whichever regional language you prefer.

How It Works

From Reading to Remembering

01

Browse

Open any book, chapter, and verse. Pick up a reading plan or find the verse of the day.

02

Add to Queue

Tap the memorize button on any verse. It enters your personal review queue.

03

Review Daily

The algorithm schedules reviews at the right intervals. No cramming required.

04

Master

Watch verses progress from Learning to Young to Mature to Mastered.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Bible Study with Steffi ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how it's used, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data needed to run the app:

  • Account info: If you create an account, we store your display name and authentication credentials through Firebase Authentication.
  • Local app data: Memorization progress, quiz scores, bookmarks, collections, and settings are stored on your device via AsyncStorage.
  • Leaderboard: If you score 5 or above in a quiz, your display name and score are stored in Firebase Firestore and visible to other users.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to operate the app — scheduling verse reviews, tracking progress, and displaying leaderboard rankings. We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and leaderboard storage. Google's privacy policy applies.
  • AI Services: Commentary translations may use AI. No personal data is sent — only Bible text.

4. Data Storage & Security

Most data lives locally on your device. Cloud data (leaderboard, account) is stored in Firebase with standard encryption.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. App content is suitable for all ages. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us.

6. Your Rights

Delete local data any time by clearing app storage or uninstalling. To remove cloud data (leaderboard scores), email us.

7. Advertising

There are no ads in this app. We don't share data with ad networks.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will appear here with an updated date.
