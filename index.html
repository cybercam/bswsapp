Features

What's Inside

Not another reading app. This one helps you actually retain what you read.

Spaced Repetition (SM2)

The algorithm schedules your next review right before you'd forget. Four modes: flashcard, fill-in-the-blank, first-letter, and full typing.

Your Mother Tongue

Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, Odia and more — alongside ESV, NKJV, and KJV English.

Parallel Bible

Compare any two versions side by side. Read in English while studying in your heart language, or cross-check translations.

Quiz & Leaderboard

Multiple choice, guess-the-reference, and verse completion. See how you rank against other believers.

Hebrew & Greek Interlinear

Tap any word for Strong's numbers, original definitions, transliteration, and morphology breakdown.

Commentary in Your Language

Chapter summaries, verse explanations, and cross-references — translated into whichever regional language you prefer.
